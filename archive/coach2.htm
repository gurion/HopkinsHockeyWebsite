Coaching Staff

Head Coach Jeremy Sarnovsky

Season Record: 12-13-2
MDCHA Playoffs: 1-1

Coach Jeremy Sarnovsky was born and raised in White Lake, MI. He began playing organized ice hockey in 1990, and later started playing goalie in 1992. From Pee Wee through Midget level hockey, Coach Sarnovsky played on many different teams throughout Metro Detroit area during all four seasons of the year, including four years as a member of the varsity team at Lakeland High School. During his junior and senior years a LHS, he received several awards, including All-Metro Detroit honors (Detroit News), All-Kensington Valley Conference honors (Milford Times), and all-area honors (Milford Times). Coach Sarnovsky also received All-KVC Academic Honors in each season he played for LHS.

Coach Sarnovsky decided to continue his ice hockey playing career while attending Oakland University (MI). In 1998 he co-founded the Oakland University club ice hockey team, and in 1999 served as the team's president, gaining entry for the team into the ACHA Division II level and the Michigan Collegiate Hockey Conference, and later attending the ACHA D-II National Tournament with the OU Grizzlies in 2000. In 2001, Coach Sarnovsky graduated with a BS in Computer Science from Oakland University, and moved on to law school at the University of Richmond. While attending law school, Coach Sarnovsky played for the UR Club hockey team for two years - the first as a forward due to the team's small number of players, and second year as a goalie. While at Richmond, he served as the team's Vice President of Team Affairs and their website administrator. During his playing career at UR, Coach Sarnovsky received All-Blue Ridge Conference First Team honors as a goaltender, as well as the Team MVP award for the 2003-2004 season. During that season, he led UR to their first ever appearance at the ACHA D-III National Tournament. During his final year of law school, and having exhausted his playing eligibility, he served as assistant coach of the UR team, and was also elected as the Commissioner for the Blue Ridge Hockey Conference. During his tenure as BRHC Commissioner, the league has seen enormous growth, as well as increased national recognition. He now servers as Secretary and Webmaster for the Mason-Dixon Collegiate Hockey Conference. Off the ice, Coach Sarnovsky is a Virginia-licensed attorney.

The 2006-07 season will mark the second season for Coach Sarnovsky behind the Blue Jay's bench. Afer a turn-around from a 2-12-3 season to a 12-13-2 season during only his first year on the job, Coach Sarnovsky looks to improve the team to above .500.
