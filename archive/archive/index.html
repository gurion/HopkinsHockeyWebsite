2008-2009 Information


Congratulations on a great season guys! Many thanks to Coach Kent Roddy for his efforts throughout the season as well as the outgoing administrative officers whose tireless work has allowed the club to thrive: Ryan "Fuzz" McLelland (President/Captain), Taig Rajpal (VP), Peter Sobel (Treasurer), and Jane O'Connor (Manager). As we turn a new leaf, please join me in welcoming our new officers: Taig Rajpal (President), Frank (VP), and Joe Leanza (Treasurer). I, Nam Pho, am taking over web hosting duties so feel free to contact me with any issues.

The Blue Jays are always looking for new recruits to join the team for the future. We are a competitive hockey team playing in the Mason Dixon Collegiate Hockey Association, and we are a member of the ACHA. Our season runs from September through February, and we play both home and away games in the MD, DC, and southern PA area. If you have any questions please contact our team president Taig Rajpal

See Action Photographs from Recent Blue Jays Games!

All home games are played at the Mimi DiPietro Ice Rink at Patterson Park in Baltimore, MD.
