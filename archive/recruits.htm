Want to Play for Johns Hopkins?

Please fill out the form below if you are interested in playing for the Blue Jays, or if you would just like more information on the team.  The form results will be mailed directly to the team, who will then contact you - so make sure you leave your contact information

In addition, if you are currently in high school and playing hockey, and would like a member of the staff to possibly come view one of your games or meet with you,  please leave your upcoming games schedule in the comments box. Please keep in mind that as we are not a varsity team, we cannot offer athletic scholarships. There are however, numerous academic scholarships available.

 Once submitted, please allow 7-10 days for a response.
