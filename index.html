Welcome to the home of the Johns Hopkins University Blue Jays Ice Hockey Team.



The Blue Jays are always looking for new recruits to join the team for the future. We are a competitive hockey team playing in the American Division of the Delaware Valley Collegiate Hockey Conference (DVCHC), and we are a member of the ACHA. Our season runs from September through February, and we play both home and away games in the MD, DE, and southern PA area. If you have any questions please contact us.

October/November 2014


Your Johns Hopkins Blue Jays are now in the heart of their regular season. After starting the season 2-0 the team dropped a couple of games and recovered in the last week of October. They are currently sitting at a record of 3-6 and are tied for 6th place in the DVCHC American Divison. This puts the Jays in playoff contention as they continue into their 5-game home stretch. Come out and support your Blue Jays! NOTE: All home games are free
